Front Desk QR SOP
One-minute Resident ID QR Verification Checklist
Printed: {{ printed_date|date:'F d, Y' }}

1. Staff Setup

  1. Log in using a staff/admin account.
  2. Open /residents/scan/test/ in browser.
  3. Keep this tab open during front desk operations.

2. Validate Resident ID QR

  1. Scan QR code or paste QR value in Scan QR Input.
  2. Click Open Resident.
  3. Confirm full name, address, and QR code in quick view.
  4. Click Visited Today to log transaction.

3. If QR Fails

  1. Use QR Diagnostic page quick actions to search resident.
  2. Open resident row and click Print ID to reprint.
  3. Verify bold printed line: QR: <code> before releasing ID.
Success log pattern: /residents/scan/<CODE>/ then /residents/quick-view/<ID>/.
This SOP is for internal barangay front desk use only.
